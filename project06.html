Rhizom 29
Magazine, collaborative work
2019

Rhizom was the university magazine of the MÃ¼nster School of Design, all articles written and designed by students. As part of the 29th and last issue I worked together with 14 other design students. The work began with the choice of the theme, the concept and the layout. Each designer was responsible for preparing his or her own journalistic work. This resulted in 14 articles on the topic of freedom ["Freiraum" in german] with editorial content and typographical implementation. My article dealt with the topic of mandatory vaccination for the flu in Germany, which was established shortly after publication.
