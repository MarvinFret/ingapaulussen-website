Ästhetik & Kommunikation No.186/187
Magazine, during internship at Fons Hickmann M23
2022

The magazine »aesthetics & communication« offers a forum for discussion that has always been on the fringes of the major public organs and has given and continues to give important impulses. The new edition of this magazine deals with the topic »Deep Storytelling« which includes the most diverse types of narratives and opens the space where words can be said, heard, felt and ordered. M23 provides the corresponding design.
